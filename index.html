ABOUT US

Chemsoft Virtual Lab is one of the Vertical of EAST Foundation INDIA and provides a platform for budding Chemical Engineers to get exposure of IT fusion with chemical Engineering, get the zeal to work on chemical Engineering software and engaging oneself to professional and Academic network in-order to patch up the gap between Academia & Corporate.
CHEMSOFT VIRTUAL LAB is a humble initiative was taken by the 4th & 3rd (NIT CALICUT 2013) year students This is a great platform where students having a zeal to work on softwareâ€™s related to CHEMICAL engineering will work as a group.
